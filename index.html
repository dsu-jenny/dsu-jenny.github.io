🛡️ Online Footprint Investigation

Case File:
Lily

Your mission is to investigate what a stranger could learn from Lily's online accounts and help identify safer choices.

🕵️ Your Mission: Lily has several online accounts. Your job is to investigate what a stranger could learn about her from public information online. Work with your partner to find clues, connect clues from different websites, decide which clues might be risky, and recommend ways Lily can stay safer online.

About Lily

Lily is a elementary school student who enjoys soccer, art, gaming, and spending time with friends. She shares parts of her life online, just like many kids her age. Your challenge is to figure out what strangers might be able to learn from her posts.

Questions to Answer

1What identity clues can you find?
2What school or grade-level clues appear?
3What activity, friend, or team clues appear?
4What location or routine clues can you connect?
5Which clue seems harmless alone but riskier when combined?
6What advice would you give Lily?
💡 Detective Tip: Real investigators rarely find every answer in one place. The trick is combining small clues from different websites. One clue might seem harmless, but several clues together can reveal much more than you expect.
